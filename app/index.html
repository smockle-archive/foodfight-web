Food Fight

A game cooked up for COMP 4970.

What is Food Fight?

Food Fight is a casual turn-based tactics game, without lots of complicated stats. Personal elements draw the player in—players can fall in love with our adorable foodstuffs. These vittles allow players to create unique strategies and play styles to win.
